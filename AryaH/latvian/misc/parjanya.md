A surviving Latvian daina (chant) to the god Perkons and his sons (= H: parjanya) with a possible marutian motif in his sons

Pērkoņam seši dēli,  
Visi seši Vāczemê.  
Lūdziet Dievu arajiņi,  
Lai nāk viens šâi zemê,  
Lai nāk viens šâi zemê,  
Šìs zemites trīcinàt,  
Šìs zemites trīcinàt,  
Šo asniņu līdzinàt.

Perkons (the thunderer) has six sons,   
All six are in Germany.   
Pray to the God, little plowmen,   
That one comes to this land,  
That one comes to this land,   
To make this earth tremble,   
To make this earth tremble,   
To straighten these sprouts.

*zeme = Skt jmA -> earth; trīcinàt  ~ trasati; dievu = deva; sesi = ShaT.
