A surviving Latvian daina (chant) to the "Mother of the Wind" (Vēja māte = vAyu-mAtR^i):

Apsagrieza Vēja māte,  
I vienadi, i otradi,  
Dieva namu slaucidama,  
Mēnestiņa istabiņu.

Mother of Vēja (Wind) turned herself,  
This way and that,  
Sweeping the house of the God,  
The little room of the Moon.  

Mēness= Moon
