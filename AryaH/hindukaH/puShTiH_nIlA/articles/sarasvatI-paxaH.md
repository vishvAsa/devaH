## जयदेवः
This verse from Gīta-Govinda may give a hint of what Kavi Jayadeva's religious affiliation was:

> वाग्-देवता-चरित-चित्रित-चित्त-सद्मा  
पद्मावती-चरण-चारण-चक्र-वर्ती ।  
श्रीवासु-देव--रति-केलि-कथा-समेतं  
एतं करोति जय-देव-कविः प्रबन्धम् ॥

> "The poet Jayadeva, whose heart-abode is adorned with the exploits of the Goddess of Speech (Sarasvatī), who is the supreme sovereign among those devoted to the feet of Padmāvatī (Lakṣmī), composes this literary work, filled with narratives of the amorous sports of Śrī Vāsudeva."
(Gīta-Govinda 1.2)
